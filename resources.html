Story of the Week

The biblical story of Abraham, found in the Book of Genesis, is foundational to Judaism, Christianity, and Islam. Abraham, originally named Abram, is considered the patriarch of these faiths. Abraham's story is one of faith, obedience, and the covenant between God and his chosen people. He is revered as a model of faith in monotheistic traditions.
