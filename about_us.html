Counseling for Children and Adolescents

At Heavenly Hands, we integrate Christian principles and values into our counseling approach, offering clients the opportunity to explore their faith journey, spiritual beliefs, and relationship with God within the therapeutic process. Our faith-based counseling services aim to provide holistic healing that encompasses the mind, body, and spirit.

We offer counseling services for individuals struggling with addiction, whether it be substance abuse, alcoholism, gambling, or other addictive behaviors. Our counselors provide personalized treatment plans, support in recovery, and guidance in navigating the spiritual dimensions of addiction.

Our team includes counselors experienced in working with children and adolescents, addressing issues such as behavioral challenges, academic struggles, family conflict, peer relationships, and emotional regulation. We provide age-appropriate counseling approaches that honor the developmental needs and unique perspectives of young clients.
